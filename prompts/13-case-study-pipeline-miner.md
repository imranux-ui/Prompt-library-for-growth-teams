## Task

[COMPOUND] Write a GrowthX case study from the raw materials provided.

Transform the raw notes, metrics, and quotes into a polished case study following the GrowthX case study format (SYSTEM_PROMPT Section 5.5). This will be published on growthx.ai/customers or shared in sales conversations.

Structure requirements:
- **Headline:** "[Client] [achieved X result] in [timeframe]" — specific metric, not vague outcome
- **The Problem:** What the client was struggling with before GrowthX. Make it feel real — the frustration, the stakes, the constraint. 2–3 paragraphs. Write from the client's perspective.
- **The Approach:** What GrowthX did. Strategy decisions, content types, workflow choices, expert vs AI balance. Be specific about *why* each decision was made. 3–4 paragraphs.
- **The Results:** 4–6 specific metrics with timeframes (e.g. "200x increase in clicks over 8 months", "10x LLM referral traffic in 3 months"). One headline result should stand alone as a pull-quote stat.
- **Customer quote:** 1–3 sentences, attributed to name and role. Must sound like a real person, not a press release. Flag as [NEEDS APPROVAL] for client to sign off.
- **What's next:** 1 paragraph on how the results are compounding and what GrowthX is expanding into next.

After the draft, provide:
- Suggested hero stat for the case study card on growthx.ai (the bold number shown in previews)
- 3 suggested social media angles to promote this case study
- Any missing data points that would strengthen the story (flag as [DATA NEEDED])
