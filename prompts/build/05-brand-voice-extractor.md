## Prompt 05 — Brand Voice Extractor
### Phase: BUILD
### Version: 1.0
#
### REQUIRES: 3–5 samples of the client's existing content (URLs or pasted text)
### OUTPUT:   Filled CLIENT_VOICE block ready to copy into CLIENT_CONTEXT.md
# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

## Task

[BUILD] Analyze the content samples provided and extract a reusable CLIENT_VOICE profile.

Read all samples carefully. Then produce:

1. **Tone description** — 3–5 adjectives with a one-sentence elaboration (e.g. "Direct and data-led — claims are backed by numbers, not enthusiasm")
2. **POV** — What perspective does this brand write from? (operator, researcher, challenger, educator, etc.)
3. **Avoid list** — 8–12 specific words or phrases this brand never uses (based on their absence in the samples or stylistic clash)
4. **Prefer list** — 8–12 words, phrases, or sentence constructions that appear consistently
5. **Sentence length** — Short / medium / long / varied, with examples from the samples
6. **CTA style** — How do they close articles or sections? (hard sell, soft ask, community invite, etc.)
7. **What makes this voice distinctive** — One paragraph a writer could read before drafting to get into character

Then output a filled CLIENT_VOICE block in the exact schema from the system prompt, ready to paste into CLIENT_CONTEXT.md.
