## Task

[EXECUTE] Audit and strengthen the provided content for pipeline conversion.

This prompt is for content that ranks or will rank — but needs to convert more readers into demo requests, trials, or contacts. Do not change the SEO structure. Focus on:

1. **CTA audit** — Is there a clear, specific CTA tied to the client's conversion goal? Is it in the right place (early and end)? Rewrite if weak.
2. **Intent bridge** — Does the article naturally transition from educating the reader to positioning the client as the solution? If not, add a transition paragraph.
3. **Social proof insertion** — Identify 1–2 places to insert a customer result, quote, or metric that builds confidence before the CTA. Write placeholder text for human to populate with real data.
4. **Objection handling** — What would stop a reader from clicking the CTA? Add a sentence or paragraph that pre-empts the most common objection.
5. **Contextual CTA placement** — Identify the highest-intent paragraph in the article (where the reader's problem is most acute) and insert a contextual inline CTA there
6. **Exit intent block** — Write a final "Is [client] right for you?" section (3–5 bullets of ICP qualifiers) before the closing CTA

Output only the changed sections with "INSERT AFTER [H2 name]" or "REPLACE [existing text]" instructions.
