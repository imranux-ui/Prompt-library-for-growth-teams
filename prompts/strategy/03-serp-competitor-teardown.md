## Task

[STRATEGY] Run a competitor teardown for the target keyword below.

Analyze the competitor content provided and produce a structured teardown that tells us exactly how to outrank and outperform it. Cover:

1. **Intent match assessment** — Does the competitor content fully satisfy the search intent? Where does it fall short?
2. **Content gaps** — What questions, angles, or sub-topics does it fail to address that a searcher would want?
3. **E-E-A-T weaknesses** — Where is the content thin on experience, expertise, authority, or trust signals?
4. **GEO score estimate** — Run a rough GEO checklist against the competitor (0–2 per item). What's their estimated score?
5. **Structural opportunities** — What format improvements (FAQ, TL;DR, comparison table, schema) could we add that they lack?
6. **Our winning angle** — A specific, differentiated approach that would make our content demonstrably better, not just longer
7. **Recommended H1 and outline** — Based on all of the above, propose an H1 and top-level H2 structure for our version
