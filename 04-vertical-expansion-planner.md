## Task

[STRATEGY] Identify the highest-value adjacent topic verticals this client should expand into next.

Based on the CLIENT_CONTEXT and existing content footprint provided, map out the next expansion plays. For each vertical, provide:

1. **Vertical name and description** — What topic territory is this? Why is it adjacent?
2. **Strategic rationale** — Why does owning this vertical compound the client's existing authority?
3. **Top 3 entry-point keywords** — The best keywords to start with in this vertical (include SV, KD, intent)
4. **ICP relevance** — Which buyer persona in the client's ICP cares about this vertical, and at what funnel stage?
5. **Competitor landscape** — Who currently owns this vertical? Are they strong or beatable?
6. **Time to rank estimate** — Rough estimate given the client's current DA and content velocity
7. **GEO opportunity** — Will LLMs cite content on this topic? Is there whitespace in AI answers?

Rank verticals by a composite score of: pipeline proximity × topical adjacency × competitive opportunity.

Output 3–5 verticals total.
